**Privacy Policy – Clean Plate**

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Clean Plate ("we", "our", or "us") provides a weekly meal planning mobile application that allows users to plan breakfast, lunch, and dinner for each day of the week.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

---

### 1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information through third-party services used in the app:

**a. Google Sign-In**
When you sign in using Google, we may receive basic profile information such as your name and email address.

**b. Firebase Services (by Google)**
We use the following Firebase services:

* **Firebase Analytics** – collects usage data such as app interactions, device information, and usage patterns.
* **Firebase Crashlytics** – collects crash reports, device state, and diagnostic data to improve app stability.

**c. Advertising (AdMob)**
We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for advertising purposes.

---

### 2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

* Enable user authentication
* Provide and improve app functionality
* Analyze usage and performance
* Fix bugs and crashes
* Display advertisements

---

### 3. Meal Planning Data

Meal plans created by users (e.g., breakfast, lunch, dinner entries) are stored locally on the user's device and are not shared with our servers.

---

### 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

Data may be processed by third-party services:

* Google Sign-In
* Firebase (Analytics and Crashlytics)
* Google AdMob

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

---

### 5. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Data collected by third-party services is retained according to their respective policies.

---

### 6. Data Security

We rely on trusted third-party providers (Google services) that implement industry-standard security measures.

---

### 7. Your Choices

You can:

* Stop using the app at any time
* Remove app access from your Google account

### 8. Children's Privacy
